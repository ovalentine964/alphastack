### 1.1 Demis Hassabis: "AGI Is Probably Only a Few Short Years Away"
- **Source:** [The Verge — Hassabis Global AI Watchdog](https://www.theverge.com/tech/965270/google-deepmind-demis-hassabis-global-ai-watchdog) (Jul 14, 2026)
- **What Happened:** Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis published a blog post titled *"A Framework for Frontier AI and the Dawning of a New Age"*, stating that AGI "is probably only a few short years away" and that we are "standing in the foothills of the singularity." He proposed a US-led global AI watchdog with the power to evaluate frontier models before release and coordinate industry-wide slowdowns if judged too risky.
- **Key Details:**
  - Hassabis has been quietly briefing the Trump administration, other AI labs, and European officials
  - Hopes to have the organization running before end of 2026
  - Says "noises from the Trump administration are very positive"
  - The organization would resemble FINRA (Financial Industry Regulatory Authority)
  - Hassabis won the 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry for AI-based protein prediction
- **AlphaStack Impact:** 🔴 **HIGH** — If AGI is "a few years away" (2028-2030), AlphaStack needs to plan for a world where AI trading edges commoditize rapidly. Current alpha sources may evaporate as AGI-level systems become widespread. Strategic planning should model scenarios where AGI arrives by 2028.

### 1.2 RSI: The New AGI Buzzword
- **Source:** [TechCrunch — RSI is the new AGI](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/28/rsi-is-the-new-agi-and-its-just-as-hard-to-pin-down/) (May 28, 2026 — ongoing discourse)
- **What Happened:** Recursive Self-Improvement (RSI) has emerged as the latest frontier concept — AI systems that can continuously upgrade themselves in a closed loop. Multiple startups (including one from well-known AI researcher Richard Socher) are explicitly pursuing RSI. The concept has replaced AGI as the key buzzword for "cataclysmic AI takeoff."
- **Key Details:**
  - RSI refers to AI systems that manage their own upgrade cycle better than humans
  - Once achieved, the process becomes a closed loop limited only by compute
  - Multiple labs now reference RSI in their roadmaps
  - The goal remains elusive — no lab has demonstrated true RSI yet
- **AlphaStack Impact:** 🟡 **MEDIUM** — RSI would represent a phase change in AI capabilities. If achieved, it would compress the AGI timeline from years to months. AlphaStack should monitor RSI progress as a leading indicator of discontinuous capability jumps that could disrupt all quantitative trading strategies simultaneously.

### 4.1 New York: First Statewide Data Center Moratorium
- **Source:** [The Verge — NY data center moratorium](https://www.theverge.com/policy/965110/new-york-ai-data-center-moratorium) (Jul 14, 2026)
- **What Happened:** Governor Kathy Hochul signed the nation's first statewide moratorium on new hyperscale data centers (over 50MW) for up to one year. State lawmakers also passed a stricter version (20MW threshold) awaiting her signature.
- **Key Details:**
  - Moratorium blocks new environmental permits for data centers over 50MW
  - Purpose: time to develop regulations protecting residents from rising energy costs and environmental impact

  - Hochul also using AI to "analyze every single rule, regulation, and policy" in the state
- **AlphaStack Impact:** 🟡 **MEDIUM** — Data center regulation could increase AI compute costs in certain jurisdictions. AlphaStack should monitor regulatory trends that could affect cloud compute pricing and availability. The trend toward regulating AI infrastructure is accelerating.
