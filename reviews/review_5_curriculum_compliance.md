## 5. COMPLIANCE RISKS NOT ADDRESSED

### 5.1 Identified but Under-Explored Risks

| Risk | Report Coverage | Gap |
|------|----------------|-----|
| **Consumer Protection Act 2012** | Not mentioned | Kenya's Consumer Protection Act applies to financial services. Unfair contract terms, misleading advertising, and inadequate service quality are actionable. Alpha Stack's ToS must comply. |
| **Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act 2018** | Not mentioned | Unauthorized access to computer systems, data interference, and cyber fraud are criminal offenses. Alpha Stack's security posture must address this. |
| **Proceeds of Crime and Anti-Money Laundering Act (POCAMLA)** | Mentioned but not detailed | Specific obligations: suspicious transaction reporting to FRC, record-keeping, customer due diligence. If Alpha Stack handles user funds or facilitates transactions, POCAMLA obligations are triggered. |
| **National Payment System Act 2011** | Not mentioned | If Alpha Stack processes payments or facilitates fund transfers, NPS Act licensing from CBK may be required. |
| **Kenya Information and Communications Act (KICA)** | Not mentioned | Regulates electronic communications. Relevant if Alpha Stack sends automated trading signals via SMS, email, or push notifications. |
| **Algorithmic Audit Requirements** | Not mentioned | While Kenya doesn't have MiFID II-style algo audit rules, as the CMA expands its perimeter, algorithmic audit requirements could be introduced. Alpha Stack should build audit trails proactively. |
| **Market Manipulation Liability** | Under-explored | If Alpha Stack's aggregated trading activity constitutes market manipulation (wash trading, spoofing, price manipulation), both the system operator and users could face CMA enforcement action. Position size limits relative to market liquidity should be enforced. |
| **Cross-Border Licensing** | Covered at high level | Each African jurisdiction has different requirements. The report mentions Nigeria, South Africa, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda but doesn't provide a licensing roadmap for each. |
| **Intellectual Property Protection** | Not mentioned | Alpha Stack's algorithms, strategies, and brand need IP protection. Kenya's Industrial Property Act and Copyright Act apply. Trademark registration for "ALPHA STACK" should be filed with KIPI (Kenya Industrial Property Institute). |
| **Employment Law (if hiring)** | Not mentioned | If Alpha Stack hires developers, analysts, or support staff, Kenya's Employment Act 2007 applies. |
